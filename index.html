Noa Hodgi (aliases noa_og & ultimemega_loser)

While comics, storyboard and character design, are the three main reasons why I decided to pursue this artistic career, my studies brought me to develop skills in character modeling as well, forging within me a newfound passion for work in volume.

After taking classes in École Georges Méliès' Fine Arts Preparatory, I graduated from a bachelor in 2D/3D animation in 2025, and am now working on various personal projects to explore all the different ways I can share my art with people.

Hello, my name is Noa!

I’m a storyteller at heart, eager to share emotions with the viewer/reader. In order to do so, I decided to use various artistic mediums... Such as writing, music, and most importantly drawing. I take every opportunity and challenge with great excitement, willing to wholeheartedly engage in any production.
